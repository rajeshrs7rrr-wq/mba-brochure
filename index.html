Comprehensive Curriculum

Our 2-year program combines foundational business knowledge with specialized expertise

  • Year 1: Accounting, Finance, Economics, Marketing, Operations, and Organizational Behavior
  • Year 2: Strategic Management, Business Analytics, Entrepreneurship, and Electives in your chosen specialization
  • Specializations: Finance, Marketing, Operations, Entrepreneurship, International Business, Healthcare Management
  • Capstone Project: Real-world consulting project with actual business clients
  • Global Immersion: International business trip and cross-cultural learning experiences
  • Professional Development: Leadership workshops, executive seminars, and networking events

Admissions Information

We seek passionate individuals committed to making an impact in the business world.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution
  • GMAT or GRE scores
  • Professional work experience (2+ years preferred)
  • Strong academic record
  • TOEFL/IELTS (for international students)

Application Process

  • Complete online application
  • Submit transcripts and test scores
  • Professional resume and essays
  • Personal interview
  • Decision notification within 4 weeks

Tuition & Financial Aid

  • Tuition: $60,000 per year
  • Scholarships available (50% of students receive aid)
  • Flexible payment plans
  • Federal loan eligibility
  • Merit-based and need-based awards
