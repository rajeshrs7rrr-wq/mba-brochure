💡 Six Pack Tips

🎯 Keys to Success:

  • Core Exercises: Crunches, planks, leg raises (3-4x per week)
  • Cardio: 3-4 cardio sessions per week (HIIT recommended)
  • Protein: Aim for 0.8-1g per pound of body weight
  • Calorie Deficit: 300-500 calories below maintenance
  • Sleep: 7-9 hours per night for recovery
  • Consistency: Stick with your plan for 12+ weeks
  • Hydration: Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily
  • Avoid: Processed foods, excess sugar, and alcohol
